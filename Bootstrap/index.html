About us

"A people without the knowledge of their past history, origin and culture is like a tree without roots."

Marcus Garvey

Parambariyam-the bloomed plant having its strong roots on the culture and heriatage of our very own TAMIL NADU.It is basically an NGO serving for the cause of "Promoting cultural and traditional practices" which are in the verge of extinct.We,as member of this prestigious place have the responsibility of protecting our cultural values and taking it safely to our next generation.But in the growth of modernity we started losing our incredible practices and forms which once stood as our identity.

Our traditional games

Our traditional games had not been played just for passing time,they created intelligent vibes for the people.The games and sports were smart,tactical and stood as a sign of bravery They remained iconic,different also helped for a healthy lifestyle.
